In Board Category


Explore excellence in construction with Deviransh Enterprise's Board Category. From robust Gypsum Boards to specialized options like Gypsum-Based Technical Boards, Hilux Calcium Silicate Boards, and versatile Cement Boards, we redefine durability. Our selection, featuring brands like USG Knauf, Gyproc, Ramco, and Everest, ensures strength and versatility in every board.

Gypsum Based Technical Board (Make: USG Knauf, and Gyproc):

Our Gypsum-Based Technical Boards, sourced from industry leaders USG Knauf and Gyproc, provide innovative building alternatives. Resistant to fire, moisture, impact, sound, and more, these boards offer rock-solid durability for a wide range of construction challenges.

Gypsum Board (Make: USG Knauf, and Gyproc):

Deviransh Enterprise proudly presents top-quality Gypsum Boards sourced from renowned manufacturers such as USG Knauf and Gyproc. These boards serve as the foundation for sturdy and versatile interior applications, offering a perfect blend of durability and design flexibility.

Hilux Calcium Silicate Board (Make: Ramco):

Deviransh Enterprise brings you the Hilux Calcium Silicate Board, crafted by Ramco. Known for its exceptional strength and resistance to fire, this board is ideal for applications requiring robust construction and enduring performance.

Cement Board (Make: Everest and Ramco):

Discover reliability with Deviransh Enterprise's Cement Boards, sourced from trusted manufacturers Everest and Ramco. Designed for both interior and exterior protection, these boards offer resistance against fire, moisture, and mold, ensuring the safety and security of your buildings.
